Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an album?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen and follow the form to set up your album's name and frequency.
Can I change the frequency of an album?
Yes, go to the album settings and choose a new frequency.
How do I add photos to an album?
Tap the album you want to add to, then tap the "Add photo" button to choose between taking a new picture or importing from your photo library.
How do I use the before and after comparison feature?
Within a photo, select another one you want to compare with. The app will automatically display them side-by-side.
Can I share my progress photos?
Absolutely! Tap the share icon on the comparison screen to share directly to social media or messaging apps.
What happens if I miss a reminder to take a photo?
You can still add the photo later. Go to the album and manually add the photo for the missed date.
Is my data backed up?
PicProgress does not currently offer cloud backup. We recommend saving your photos to your device's photo library as well.
